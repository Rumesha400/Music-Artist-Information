Shaan

About:

Since the mid-90's, Shaan has been lending his warm, evocative vocals to Indian pop and filmi smashes while simultaneously pursuing a successful career as TV host for popular music shows. Shaan honed his Indian pop chops on intriguing solo releases. Aksar... (2004) includes a remix of his turn-of-the-century hit “Tanha Dil” with vocals by the Spice Girls' Mel C. Shaan won the Filmfare Award for Best Male Playback Singer twice: in 2007 for “Chand Sifarish” from Fanaa, alongside Kailash Kher, and in 2008 for “Jab Se Tere Naina” from Saawariya.

Atif Aslim

About:

Aslam is best-known for his prolific work as a playback singer, recording vocals for tracks that Bollywood actors then lip sync to onscreen. While still in college, Atif Aslam formed the rock band Jal and financed the recording of their hit single “Aadat”. The success of “Aadat” drew the attention of Bollywood director Mahesh Bhatt, who asked Aslam to record a track for the film Zeher.

KK

About:

Delhi native KK—short for Krishnakumar Kunnath—is a popular and prolific Bollywood playback singer. His 1999 solo debut, Pal, spawned the hit power ballad “Pyaar Ke Pal,” which remained a top-selling song in 2020 on India's iTunes Top 100 Alternative Songs chart. At the 2009 Screen Awards, which honor excellence in Indian films, KK won Best Male Playback Singer for “Khuda Jaane,” from the movie Bachna Ae Haseeno. In 2012, he won the Eenam-Swaralaya Singer of the Year Award for his Malayalam language songs.

Salim Merchant

About:

Singer, composer and producer Salim Merchant bridges generations, styles and themes with a honed, versatile sonic sensibility that he's championed in film music and beyond. He's perhaps best known for his work with his older brother Sulaiman Merchant as Salim-Sulaiman. The duo have put their powerful spin on Bollywood hits like “Ainvayi Ainvayi” (from Band Baaja Baaraat) and “Chak De! India”, on fusion on songs for Coke Studio India and their own collaborative song series Bhoomi, as well as on spiritual songs, pop and progressive rock.Having also co-founded Merchant Records, his production skills shine through on everything from wide-ranging fusion to pop to rock.
